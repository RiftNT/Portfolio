SakYahe: A Mobile-based Carpool System

A mobile-based carpool platform designed to provide an efficient and eco-friendly transportation solution for students.

Code Link

Project Overview

The system allows students to find and join carpool groups with fellow students traveling to the same destination, reducing the number of vehicles on the road and promoting social connections among students. It features a user-friendly interface that enables students to create and manage their profiles, search and join existing carpool groups, and communicate with group members. Moreover, it also includes safety features, such as driver verification and ratings

Overall, the mobile-based carpool system offers a convenient and sustainable transportation alternative for students, while promoting social interactions and reducing traffic congestion.
